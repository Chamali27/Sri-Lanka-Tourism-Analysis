# 🇱🇰 Sri Lanka Tourist Arrivals Analysis (2020–2025) | Power BI Project

## Project Overview
This project presents a data-driven analysis of Sri Lanka tourist arrivals from 2020 to 2025 using official SLTDA data.

The objective is to study tourism recovery after COVID-19, identify key source markets, and analyze seasonal travel patterns using interactive Power BI dashboards.

## Objectives
- Analyze tourist arrival trends in Sri Lanka (2020–2025)
- Study COVID-19 impact and recovery patterns
- Identify top source countries and regional distribution
- Understand seasonal variations in tourism demand
- Build interactive dashboards for decision-making

---

## Data Source
All data was obtained from official publications of the **Sri Lanka Tourism Development Authority (SLTDA)**:

- Annual Statistical Reports (2020–2024)
- Year-in-Review Reports
- Monthly Excel datasets (2020–2025)

The dataset includes:
- Monthly tourist arrivals
- Country-wise breakdown (234 countries)
- Regional classifications
- Annual summaries

## 📈 Key Insights
- Strong recovery after COVID-19 pandemic (2022 onwards)
- India remains the #1 source market throughout 2020–2025
- Europe contributes over 50% of total arrivals
- December is consistently the peak tourism month
- May records the lowest arrivals due to seasonal effects

---

## Key Findings
- Tourism industry collapsed during 2020–2021 due to COVID-19
- Rapid recovery observed from 2022 onwards
- High dependency on a few key markets (India + Europe)
- Strong seasonality in tourist arrivals
